Our aim with this guide is to show you how to get the pet you've always wished for without violating any laws or ethical principles.
We know the thrill that comes with owning a wild animal, and the excitement of sharing it on social mediaSome of the most popular Instagram accounts in the UAE showcase exotic animals for all your followers to see.
But national and international lawsIn 2017, the UAE passed an animal welfare law banning wild pets are in place as well as pressure from conservationists and animal rights groups, to stop the smuggling and trafficking of exotic species.

To support humane dealing : Run a background check on your dealer to guarantee that they are not engaging in cruel and/or unethical activites.








Cheetahs transported from the Horn of Africa Many come from Somaliland, one of the poorest countries in the world to the Gulf are often carried in tight, dark crates without proper nourishment. Given that many cubs die during the journey, the survivors remain inside the crates next to their dead siblings for long periods of time.



To support humane trafficking : Require photographic evidence of the cheetah's comfortable journey, as well as documents to prove that it was transported on an Etihad first-class seat, before purchasing it.
